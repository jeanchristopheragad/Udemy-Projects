About Me

Hi, I’m Jean Christopher Agad — I go by Jean. I’m 22 years old and currently an Executive Partner at Athena, supporting a fintech company based in Colombia. My background spans administrative support, BPO operations, and client management, but at my core, I’m driven by something deeper: mastery. I’m passionate about programming, mathematics, and science, and I aim to become someone who can understand and work across programming languages with confidence and depth. Whether I’m supporting a client, collaborating with colleagues, or helping someone solve a problem, I bring analytical thinking, curiosity, and a constant desire to learn.

My journey hasn’t been linear. I was introduced to computer science in high school, though I only truly immersed myself in it later on. I began college studying BS Mathematics, but financial challenges forced me to pause my education. Instead of stopping, I adapted — working in telecommunications and travel BPO as a customer service representative before finding my role at Athena. Seeing my peers graduate and thinking seriously about my future pushed me to pursue certifications and reconsider finishing my bachelor’s degree, potentially shifting to Computer Science. Overcoming financial hardship shaped my independence and resilience. Today, I’m proud not just of where I am, but of the persistence it took to get here.

Outside of work, I’m someone who believes life doesn’t always need to be overly formal — sometimes you just need to freestyle and keep moving. I care deeply about animals, value quality and efficiency in everything I do, and approach challenges with diligence. I enjoy video games, quiet downtime, and spending time with cats and dogs. When people interact with me, I want them to feel that I’m approachable and easy to work with — but also principled, capable, and not someone who can be taken lightly.
